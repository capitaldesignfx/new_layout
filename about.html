About Us

Techorrect Inc. offers test automation and development acceleration consulting for software development projects. We help companies set up systems and processes that ensure quality of their software allowing for uninterrupted productivity for their developers. As well as offering test automation plans and their implementation, we support many aspects of software development projects, modernizing the development and testing environments in efficient and proactive ways.
